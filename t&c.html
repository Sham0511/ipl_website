Match Playing Conditions

These are the rules and guidelines that govern how IPL matches are conducted. They cover aspects like pitch conditions, playing hours, rain delays, and more.

For detailed information, you can download the PDF document.

IPL Players' Contract

Each player is initially hired for a period of three years. The total fees a player receives are based on the amount for which they were bought during the auction. If a team decides to release a player, they still need to honor the contract and pay the player for the contracted term.

Ticket Terms and Conditions

IPL tickets come with specific terms and conditions. When you purchase a ticket, it entitles you to attend a match at the stadium. If there are multiple matches scheduled in the stadium on the same day, the ticket is valid for all those matches.

IPL 2024 Rules and Regulations

The IPL 2024 season will be played in a home-and-away format. A total of 74 matches will be played among the 10 participating teams during the season. The tournament will have a group stage and playoff format.
